Jhon Ramirez | Mon 21 August 2017

What's up!

This is the next challenge in my Android Course, I need to think an idea for an app, and that app will be the project I will work through all the course!

Here is the repo for the project. And here is a zip with the files for this challenge.

For this reason, my team and me started to think in daily problems that people have, and we found great ideas to solve those problems. For example, What if we develop an app that helps people to...

  • Know if their children or pets go out outside of a safe zone.
  • Send a print order to a Print Center and pay through your phone when you get there.
  • Order a table and a choose your meal when you are near a restaurant so you don't have to wait.
  • And many others.

At the end, we chose to build an app that helps people, who buy and sell goods, to manage their inventory in an easy and useful way.

Introducing you Inventorify an app that allows you to:

  1. Manage all the stuff you want to sell.
  2. Know when to refill your warehouse.
  3. Take better decisions based in analytics.

With Inventorify you can make your business to increase its sales and to grow like you always have dreamed.

I hope you like the development process for this app :)
